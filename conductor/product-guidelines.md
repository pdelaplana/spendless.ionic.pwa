# Product Guidelines: Spendless

## Tone and Voice
- **Empathetic & Calm:** Our communication should be supportive and non-judgmental. We avoid the "guilt trip" often associated with budgeting.
- **Supportive Prose:** Use encouraging language, especially when discussing financial goals or unexpected expenses.
- **Clarity and Simplicity:** While empathetic, the language must be clear and direct to ensure users understand their financial status at a glance.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Efficiency First:** The core "Add Expense" flow must be lightning-fast (target: 10 seconds) to ensure tracking doesn't become a chore.
- **Mindful Interaction:** Despite being fast, the UI should provide brief moments for reflection, particularly when selecting a mindful category.
- **Universal Access:** All features must function identically and performantly across the PWA, iOS, and Android versions.
- **Consistency:** Use established Ionic components and patterns to provide a familiar and reliable experience.

## Design and Visual Style
- **Established Spendless Palette:** Adhere strictly to the defined brand colors and design system to maintain a cohesive identity.
- **Subtle Feedback:** Use micro-interactions, like haptics and soft animations, to provide positive reinforcement for completed tasks.
- **Category Clarity:** Use consistent icons and color-coding for the six mindful categories to help users build quick visual associations.

## Communication and Feedback
- **Helpful Errors:** When something goes wrong, provide clear, actionable, and non-blaming error messages that guide the user to a solution.
- **Proactive Insights:** AI-driven insights should be presented as gentle "nudges" or reflections, not as rigid commands or criticisms.
- **Contextual Help:** Provide simple tooltips or help text within the app to assist users in understanding mindful tracking concepts.
